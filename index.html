Tileify • Classroom Friendly Version

Learn algebra as movement.

This version keeps the full Tileify engine and adds a Literal Mode for equations like F = ma. Students can enter equations like F = ma, then move symbolic factors such as m or a into denominator position.

Mobile tip: Tileify works best in landscape mode so the tiles can stay in a row.
Quick Start

How students should use this

  1. Enter a math expression or equation in the box below.
  2. Drag matching like terms together, drag across the boundary, or pick a transformation button.
  3. Watch the tiles snap into place and look for patterns. On phones, landscape mode keeps the tile row easier to read.
  4. Check Branch Results whenever a split happens.

Tileify is built to support discovery. Students do not have to know the whole procedure in advance — they can see the legal moves and investigate what happens.

Step 2

Work with the tiles

The tile field comes first. Drag a tile across the boundary, or use the buttons directly below the tiles.

Drag mode: drag a tile onto a matching like term to combine. Drag a whole tile across the boundary for additive movement. Drag the small outside-factor handle to the other side’s denominator drop zone for denominator movement.
