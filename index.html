Tileify • Classroom Friendly Version

Learn algebra as movement.

This version keeps the full Tileify engine, keeps tile properties hidden until right-click, and improves the tile layout for smaller screens. Students can enter an equation, explore suggested moves, and discover how algebra works step by step. Tile properties stay hidden unless revealed.

Mobile tip: Tileify works best in landscape mode so the tiles can stay in a row.
Quick Start

How students should use this

  1. Enter a math expression or equation in the box below.
  2. Pick a suggested move from the action area.
  3. Watch the tiles change and look for patterns. On phones, landscape mode keeps the tile row easier to read.
  4. Check Branch Results whenever a split happens.

Tileify is built to support discovery. Students do not have to know the whole procedure in advance — they can see the legal moves and investigate what happens.
